Recent Projects

A few of my most recent projects.

Snow Questions: An elegantly designed front-end application that displays up-to-date weather information for popular ski resorts in Colorado. Technologies: OpenWeather API, JavaScript, jQuery, Local Storage... 

Snow Questions

HOIST: It's not LYFT, it's not UBER. IT'S HOIST. A mobile ride-share app designed to connect people with FREE rides. The internet's guide to the hitch hiking, as we call it. With Hoist, users can either offer or request a ride through their unique profile. Focusing on the concert-culture Hoist pulls API data for local concerts and connects users with people who are going to the same show! Part economy and part social media this app is designed to do one thing. Help people hitch a ride to concerts or wherever! Technologies: React/Redux, PostgreSQL, Node, JamBase API (STILL IN PRODUCTION, CLICK LINK)

CloudVu: In the mountains, weather changes fast & can vary drastically between say, the North and South face of a hill. You dont want to find yourself half way up a pitch just in time to see the thunderheads roll in. Weather forcasts are visualized with a D3 stream graph so that the user can see current & future-cast trends in the weather. Whether you are on your local hiking trails or half-way up Everest, CloudVu is a full-stack app that takes the guess work out of ever-changing weather conditions. Securely log-in to save your favorite spots or simply view the map, point/click and get current and future weather information for any spot on the globe. Technologies: D3, Mapbox API, PostgreSQL, Node, Express...
