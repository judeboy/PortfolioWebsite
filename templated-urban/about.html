MY STORY

Judah Trimmer

Here is a little more info

I grew up in Illinois. I come from a big family and love being around other people. After a five-year enlistment in the Air Force I came to Boulder and attended CU. My travels and experiences during my militar service completely altered the way I viewed myself and the role I played as a member of the human race. I began to ask myself: What will I do with my time on Earth? How will I achieve my goals? What will my impact be?

As I happily move through life, I allow these considerations to guide me. Conservation of the planet. Increasing quality of life for other people. Pursing passion projects like music, art, and travel. My dream job would grant me the opportunity explore those possiblities via web development and app creation.
